About Our College

Our History

Founded in 1985, City College has been a beacon of knowledge for over three decades. We started with just 50 students and have grown into a premier institution.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality education that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and ethical leadership in a diverse global community.
